Thanks for downloading my app!

This app contains multiple related YouTube channels to make it easy to watch videos that interest you. YouTube has a great app, but it can be overwhelming to deal with dozens of subscriptions, navigating around and waiting for YouTube to load it's content. This app aims to be simple with no surprises. Just click the videos you want to watch. No ads, no junk, just videos.

This app caches most information so it's fast!

Quick start

  • Change channels using the popup menu in the action bar
  • Swipe to hide cards
  • To unhide a card, choose "Show hidden items" in the action menu. Then click to unhide any cards previously hidden
  • Use the slide out drawer to navigate sections of the current channel
  • Pull down the list to refresh
